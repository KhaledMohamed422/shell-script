<h2>Start Here</h2>
<p>Welcome to the hub for Zapier AI Actions! Before you dive in, read about three important concepts below.</p>
<hr />
<div class="grid">
    <div class="grid-item">
        <img src="https://cdn.zappy.app/88dca0b3afc306dd6c8d2503791427a4.png" />
    </div>
    <div class="grid-item">
        <h4>About AI Actions</h4>
        <p>AI Actions is a tool for builders to equip AI platforms (or custom integrations) the ability to run any Zapier action! The 20,000+ searches and actions you know and love from the <a href="https://zapier.com/apps">Zapier automation platform</a> can be used with your favorite AI platform.</p>
    </div>
</div>
<hr />
<h3>1. How it works</h3>
<div class="grid">
    <div class="grid-item">
        <p><a href="https://zapier.com/" target="_blank">Zapier</a> is an integration platform that hosts 5,000+ third-party apps and has 30,000+ actions. AI Actions equips your own AI or systems with the ability to run any action on Zapier (eg. Send a Slack message, find a Google Calendar event, draft a Gmail reply, create a HubSpot lead, etc.)</p>
        <p>In this system, your natural language command acts as the trigger and AI helps to execute the Action.</p>
        <p>Traditional hurdles like third-party app authentication and API integrations are all taken care of out of the box.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="grid-item">
        <img src="https://cdn.zappy.app/b18ecc801292bc3930165f3a89c41b51.png" />
    </div>
</div>
