            <h4 class="h4 item_mod-title">Champion minimalism.</h4>

        </div>

        
        <div class="title37-item ">
          
            <h4 class="h4 item_mod-title">Focus on the user (experience).</h4>
          

          
            <div class="item_mod-text"><p><span>Keep strong typography and visual hierarchy at the forefront of your mind. Be forever conscious of our end user — how would they use, read, or interpret this design? When and where? Why?</span></p></div>
