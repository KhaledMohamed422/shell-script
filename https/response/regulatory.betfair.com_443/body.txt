Betfair has sought to lead the gambling sector in the drive for compliance, social responsibility and integrity, three of its core values, since its inception. Betfair believes in providing an open and effective method of communicating with, and providing information for, its regulators, associated industry bodies and sporting Memoranda of Understanding (MOU) partners. <br/>
<br/>
The Compliance Portal will enhance communications between Betfair and our regulators, strengthening the ability to share information and reports in a timely manner, and providing flexibility to meet new and developing information requirements. <br/>
<br/>
The Social Responsibility Portal provides an effective method of communicating with other key industry bodies, it will allow collaboration and ultimately the advancement of Industry standards in research, treatment and prevention of problem gambling. <br/>
<br/>
The Integrity Portal will enhance communications between Betfair and our sporting MOU partner community, on a two way basis, strengthening the ability to share betting integrity related information in a timely manner. <br/>
<br/>
This portal is designed for ease of use and is based on familiar technology and methods. <br/>
