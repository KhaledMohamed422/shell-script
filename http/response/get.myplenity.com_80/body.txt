  <h2>Learn more about Gelesis loss of weight (GLOW) clinical study</h2>
  
  <p class="lead">In the GLOW study, nearly 6 out of 10 people who took Plenity lost at least 5% of their body weight. Their average weight loss was 10% (about 22 pounds). Plenity or placebo was given to 436 adults who were overweight or had obesity, with and without type 2 diabetes, over 24 weeks in conjunction with diet and exercise. The study was designed to measure: whether at least 35% of individuals receiving Plenity lost 5% of their body weight, whether individuals receiving Plenity lost 3% more of their body weight than individuals receiving placebo. Individuals on Plenity lost on average 6% vs individuals on placebo, who lost on average&nbsp;4%.</p>
